Visual artist with a career in graphic design, exploring creative coding, and web development

As an artist, I am interested the intersection of creative coding, digital art, and traditional art mediums, weaving these elements together to craft dynamic and innovative works.

Over the years, I’ve work in a variety of creative projects, including:

  • Print Design: Crafting visually compelling book covers and magazine layouts, focusing on storytelling through composition and typography.
  • Brand Identity: Designing unique logos that reflect a brand's personality and establish cohesive visual identities.
  • Digital Media: Creating animations and web designs that merge functionality with creativity for engaging user experiences.

In the past, I’ve also dabbled other creative paths, such as:

  • Stage and lighting design, where I focused on creating props for various productions, as assistant designer.
  • Puppetry theatre group during my student years, bringing stories to life through artistic expression.

I’m passionate about discovering ways to merge hands-on artistic practices with technology, aiming to push the boundaries of what’s possible in both digital and tangible forms of expression.
